About Us

At YUDI, we want to provide everyone with the opportunity to learn about finance and investing. Through consistent education meetings, a guest speaker series and sponsored stock game competition, and exclusive recruiting and career development opportunities, we invite the Yale undergraduate student body to join our journey in learning the essentials of investment analysis and valuation. We look forward to working with you to make finance and investment education accessible, fun, and rewarding at Yale. Welcome to YUDI!

Education

Education meetings cover various financial topics and are separated by each class year. Lectures are geared to help our members learn the basics of finance in a progressive manner. We call this process the YUDI Ladder. Our goal is to provide students with this platform to prepare themselves for internship and interview opportunities in the future. From basic background knowledge, to in-depth financial analysis and valuation methodologies, we are excited to delve deeper into finance with you!


SPEAKERS

Our guest lecture series supplements the group's understanding of financial markets and job opportunities. Both professionals within the industry and professors at the Yale School of Management have come to discuss different topics with our members. Speakers have included individuals within Goldman Sachs, Morgan Stanley, J.P. Morgan, and more. Our speakers discuss everything from Investment Banking, Private Equity, Investment Management, and more! Members in the past have found these events really engaging and worthwhile!


COMPETE

We host an annual mock-stock competition, through the Market Watch platform, so members can apply what they are learning and manage their own portfolio to find investments they think are worthwhile. Starting in the Fall of 2015, this year-long competition will include teams from across the Ivy-League. Winners are awarded with significant cash prizes as well as individual NYC trips, dinners, and recruiting events. YUDI members make teams of 1 - 4 individuals and represent Yale in this competition. Go Bulldogs!


CONNECT

Each semester we host firm-wide events in NYC to help our members learn more about the world of finance and connect with professionals. Our corporate sponsors are eager to meet us and we are excited for YUDI members to take advantage of these events, network, and use their experiences to shape what they are interested in and how they approach the application process in general. With corporate sponsorships from some of the largest Wall Street firms, being a member of YUDI can really set you apart!
